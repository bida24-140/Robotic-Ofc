Emmanuel Ketlogetswe (known by his stage name Robotic ofc) is an Afro Tech producer and DJ from Molepolole, Botswana. Born on November 17th, 2003, he started producing and DJing at the age of 18. With a passion for electronic music and a desire to share his unique sound with the world, Robotic has been performing regularly in local venues, gaining a devoted following along the way. His style is a fusion of traditional African rhythms and modern electronic music, characterized by deep basslines, tribal drums, and hypnotic melodies. His style is energetic and groove-driven, designed to keep the crowd dancing all night long.
